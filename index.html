About

Hello, my name is David from Toulouse, France.
I started a vocational training in 2018 to become a web developer. Graduated since 2019 and as a fullstack web developer fond of Web Security, I do now, provide my services remotely as a Freelance.

Passing on and learning inspires me, I can say without hesitation that I am lucky because in this job, you have to constantly challenge yourself and adapt, and honestly I love it. This is why I devote time once a week to immerse myself in a technical watch on promising themes, I correct my mistakes and above all I learn. I am not afraid to ask questions on points that I do not know, because I can only improve. But also, from time to time, I like to share what I have learned in the form of articles or Master Classes. Above all, I like to discuss topics that have meaning, to imagine solutions that are both sustainable and innovative. I believe that Apps should be Accessible - Secure & Eco-friendly.

An idea ? A project ? Or a need to share about web development ? Feel free to reach out.
